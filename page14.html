There’s a lot to like in Jonstown. As you can see it’s a busy place with the downtown on two levels, factories and a river port. The layout is simple: 2 separate loops. The upper loop as an oval while the lower is a figure 8. The majority of the buildings are lighted. There are numerous sight gags and humorous scenes. Hundreds of figures populate the layout as well as dozens of automobiles and trucks. Most of the businesses are named for employees of Caboose and friends. As you may know, Caboose Hobbies closed after over 70 years in Denver. It will reopen under new ownership, but this is a piece of  Model Train history. I do not have access to the layout as I am in Texas, but I am in contact with the owner.
