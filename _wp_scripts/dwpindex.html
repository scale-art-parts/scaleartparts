It has been a couple of years since these pages have been available on our site. We had gone through several changes in web site structure and unfortunately this journal was not moved or set up to reflect the new look. I am first and foremost a modeler. Getting a site up on the web and keeping it up to date was to fall to other persons, but as often happens, our plans got sidetracked. We actually thought this project had come to it’s conclusion and was/is unfinished and no progress was made for a year reflecting the wishes of the client. We were sad to think this incredible railroad would never see the light of day, but one day a letter arrived from the client and asked that we resume work although at a slower pace. So, we are back to work on the DWP. Fewer trips and shorter stays; this is not a local project for us and involves some travel. We are working toward a goal for an open house to occur sometime in 2015.

Update will be posted in the Builders Blog, but I thought some of you might enjoy seeing the old pages and having a look at where we’ve been and where we’re headed with this layout.

Enjoy the journal.....Scott
