OCW1  Concrete wall section

6”W X 11”H .090” thick

2/pk.

These wall section are cast to represent formed concrete that was formed with planks. They have a well defined uneven surface and can be used in a variety of ways:

Retaining walls

Foundation walls

Bridge abutments

Culverts to name a few.

The .090” thick casting is easy to cut or machine to fit your needs.

Use these sheet in place of sheet styrene for a more interesting appearance.

Above, These two bridge abutments were made by cutting pieces of the concrete wall sheets and cementing them to a wood support.
