I have been working on a set of F Scale Roundhouse Doors!
They are based on the doors found in the Ridgway Roundhouse on the Rio Grande Southern narrow gauge Railway. I sized them up a bit to accommodate the K series locomotives. These are companion pieces to my Roundhouse Windows. The doors have cast in board and NBW detail. They come with inner “skins” that give the doors authentic detail open and closed. I am in the process of finalizing instructions and shooting photos for the catalog. These are very neat pieces and will sell for about $30 per set, 2 doors for one bay.
